Our Story

Ek thandi raat mein ek ladka ek khali notebook ke saamne baitha tha aur bola –

“Ek din mera naam matter karega…
Ek din mera taste duniya mein jayega…”

Woh ladka tha Amresh Chaudhary.
Aaj wohi ladka Shree Chaudhary’s Rustic Royale Bakery ka founder hai.

12th fail hua, Delhi gaya, wahan dekha degree waale bhi line mein khade hain.
Usne decide kiya – “Mujhe naukri nahi, mastery chahiye.”

4 saal Delhi ki organic bakery mein haath kaale kiye, pasina bahaya, har technique seekhi.
Phir ghar lauta Khalilabad, aur yahan ek aisa taste create kiya jo ab log bhool nahi paate.

Yeh bakery nahi, ek sapna hai jo sach ho raha hai –
struggle se legacy tak ka safar.
