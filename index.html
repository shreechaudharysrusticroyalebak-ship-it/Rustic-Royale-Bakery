Our Story

Ek thandi raat, Sant Kabir Nagar mein… ek ladka khali notebook dekhta raha aur bola:

“Ek din mera naam matter karega… mera taste duniya mein jayega…”

Woh ladka tha Amresh Chaudhary. 12th fail hua, Delhi gaya, dekha degree waale bhi line mein khade hain. Usne socha – “Mujhe naukri nahi, mastery chahiye.”

4 saal Delhi ki organic bakery mein seekha, phir ghar lauta Khalilabad. Aaj wohi sapna hai Shree Chaudhary’s Rustic Royale Bakery – struggle se legacy tak ka safar.

Har bite mein honesty, har product mein passion.
